About John Wake

John Wake is a bestselling crime and thriller author whose writing is inspired by personal experiences, including the loss of his beloved dog. His stories combine suspense, mystery, and emotional depth, captivating readers worldwide.

His novels have been praised for their gripping plots, unexpected twists, and complex characters. With awards and a loyal fan base, John continues to bring thrilling narratives to crime and action readers.

"I write stories that keep the reader on edge, while exploring the complexities of human nature and the shadows we all hide." – John Wake

When he’s not writing, John enjoys exploring the streets of Detroit, drawing inspiration from everyday life, and sharing his passion for storytelling with fans through talks, workshops, and live events.

Over the years, his work has expanded to include in-depth character studies, psychological tension, and social commentary, giving readers both entertainment and reflection.
